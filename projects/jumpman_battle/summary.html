Jumpman Battle

This was a final project for a course entitled, "The Game Development Process." It was done with a team of three other people. It's a two player game that allows the player to choose from 4 different characters and 3 different stages. During battle, there are 4 different power-ups that spawn that players can pick up: invincibility, double damage, cloak (which reduces the chance of being hit), and health restoration.

I worked with other members of the team to code the fighting mechanics and stage mechanics. We used GameMaker2, which uses its own language, GameMaker Language, for game scripts.
