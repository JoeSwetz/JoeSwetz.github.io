About Me

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! My name is Joe, and I currently work as a Software Engineer for Juniper Networks, where I work on the application services team for the Session Smart Router (SSR).

Alongide my passion for computer networks and programming in general, I've always wanted to one day work as a game programmer! I have a passion for both games, and the experiences and relationships they can create. Games like Destiny 2 and Super Smash Bros are not only good games in their own right, but also serve as a means for bringing people together and creating friendships. I believe that games, as a medium, have an extraordinary ability to bring people together through play. Throughout my life, I've witnisseed this firsthand - whether it be through how my siblings and I bonded over Super Smash Bros, or through the large community surrouding Destiny 2 which, to this day, continues to participate in various charity and social justive initiaizes. I want to become a game programmer to help create these experieces, which have been so influential to me, for others.

The works on this website make up my portfolio of games and software projects that I have completed so far. I always consider it to be a work in progress, as I'm always looking to work on new projects and develop more game development skills alongside my current career as a software developer!
